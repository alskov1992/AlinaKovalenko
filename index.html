About me

Science translated into industry decisions

I am a researcher focused on shipping decarbonization and Arctic maritime operations. My work connects emissions modelling, alternative fuels, lifecycle assessment, regulatory analysis and operational risk in cold-region shipping.

I collaborate with maritime companies, ports, energy companies, public institutions and research consortia on applied projects where scientific evidence must become strategy, investment logic and implementation roadmap.

Collaboration areas

  • Decarbonization strategy and transition roadmaps
  • LNG, ammonia, hydrogen and e-fuels scenarios
  • Arctic route risk and climate adaptation
  • IMO / EU ETS / maritime regulation analysis
